Privacy Policy — Gridlock (Halo Column Control)

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Gridlock does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal or user data. It contains no analytics, tracking, telemetry, advertising, or external network requests of any kind.

What the extension stores

Gridlock saves your chosen table column-width preferences — column header names, pixel widths, and the numeric Halo list identifiers taken from the page URL — locally on your own device using the browser's chrome.storage.local API. This information never leaves your device and is never sent to the developer or any third party.

What the extension does not access

Gridlock does not read or store your browsing history, credentials, ticket contents, customer data, or any personally identifiable information. It does not communicate with any server.

Permissions

The extension runs only on halopsa.com pages, where its content script reads the ticket table's column layout and applies your saved width settings. The storage permission is used solely to save your column-width preferences locally on your device, as described above. No other permissions are requested, and no data is transmitted off your device.

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL.
